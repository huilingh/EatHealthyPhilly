What is heart smart corner stores?

Some corner stores offer monthly heart screenings for blood pressure and height and weight checks, hearty taste tests, nutrition education, and coupons to use on heart hearty foods. You can lick on the stores to find out more information.

If you want to know how to get to a heart smart corner store, you can click MY LOCATION on the navigation bar, and then click on a corner store on the map. A driving route will be displayed on the map.
