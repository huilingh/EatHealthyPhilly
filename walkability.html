Food providers in Philadelphia

Food providers in Philadelphia include grocery stores, corner stores & resteraunts* and sidewalk cafe. All data come from the business licenses data on OpenDataPhilly. The food providers are displayed on the map according to their food-related business licenses, and their legal names are shown when clicked.

*Note: It may take a longer time to proceed when you select the corner stores & resteraunts since this is a large dataset.
